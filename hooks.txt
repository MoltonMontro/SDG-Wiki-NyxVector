Hooks provided by the NyxVector skin.

'NyxVectorAfterToolbox': Called after rendering the toolbox portlet (div#p-tb).
You can use this to inject items (such as ads or a hit counter, for example)
from an extension.
This is the NyxVector couterpart to the MonoBookAfterToolbox hook in the MonoBook
skin.

'NyxVectorBeforeFooter': Called before the footer (div#footer) is output, but after
div#mw-navigation has been closed.
You can use this to inject items (such as ads or a hit counter, for example)
from an extension.
